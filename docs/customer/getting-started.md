# Getting Started — Your First 30 Minutes

Welcome to the POS system! This guide walks you through everything you need to do in your first session.

## Step 1: Complete Your Store Profile (5 min)
Go to Settings → POS Configuration.
- Set your currency symbol position
- Set your default tax settings
- Add your store address (required for receipts and compliance)

## Step 2: Add Your Products (10 min)
Go to Products → New Product.
Required fields:
- **Name**: Your product name as it appears on the receipt
- **SKU**: Your internal product code (or leave blank to auto-generate)
- **Selling Price**: What you charge customers
- **Cost Price**: What you paid (used for profit reports)
- **Category**: Helps organize your POS screen
- **Track Stock**: Turn ON if you want stock level warnings

[SCREENSHOT: Product creation form]

## Step 3: Set Opening Stock (5 min)
After adding products, go to Inventory → Set Opening Stock.
Search for each product and enter how many units you currently have.

[SCREENSHOT: Stock adjustment form]

## Step 4: Add Your First Customer (2 min)
Go to Customers → Add Customer.
At minimum: Name + Phone number.
Phone number lets you find them quickly at the POS.

## Step 5: Make Your First Sale (5 min)
Go to Dashboard → POS.
1. Search or scan a product
2. Click to add to cart
3. Click "Pay"
4. Enter cash amount → "Complete Sale"
5. Print receipt

[SCREENSHOT: POS screen with cart]

🎉 Your first sale is done!
