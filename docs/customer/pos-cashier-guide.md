# POS Cashier Guide

Everything your cashiers need to know to operate the POS.

## Starting a Shift
1. Open the POS screen: Dashboard → POS (or use the installed tablet app)
2. The POS shows your product grid. Products are sorted alphabetically.

## Making a Sale

### Adding Products
**By clicking:** Tap any product card to add it to the cart.
**By barcode:** Scan the product barcode — it adds automatically.
**By search:** Type the product name or SKU in the search bar.

[SCREENSHOT: POS screen with search highlighted]

### Adjusting Quantities
- Tap + or - to adjust quantity in the cart
- Tap the quantity number to type a custom amount (for weighted products)
- Tap the trash icon to remove an item

### Attaching a Customer
Press **F3** or tap "Walk-in" at the top of the cart to attach a customer.
Search by phone number, name, or customer code.

Why attach a customer?
- Loyalty points are earned automatically
- Outstanding credit balance is visible
- Sale appears in customer history

### Applying a Discount
Press the **Disc** button below the cart totals.
- **Percent**: 10% off entire sale
- **Fixed**: Rs 500 off entire sale
- Add a reason (for reporting)

### Payment
Press **Pay** (or F5) to open the payment screen.

**Cash:** Enter amount received → see exact change to give
**Card:** Select Card, enter amount → no change
**Split payment:** Click "Add payment method" to split across multiple methods
**On credit:** Available if customer has a credit limit set

Click **Complete Sale** to finalize.

## Offline Mode

The POS works without internet. [Full offline guide →](offline-mode.md)

Key differences in offline mode:
- Sale reference shows as OFF-XXXXXX-000001
- JazzCash and Easypaisa are not available
- Sales sync automatically when connection returns
