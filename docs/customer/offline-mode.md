# Offline Mode Guide

Your POS tablet can complete sales even without internet.

## What Works Offline
✅ Product search and barcode scanning
✅ Adding customers (from cached list)
✅ Building cart and applying discounts
✅ Cash, card (manual), on-credit payments
✅ Printing receipts (shows OFFLINE marker)

## What Requires Internet
❌ JazzCash and Easypaisa payments
❌ Creating new customers
❌ Accessing reports
❌ Inventory management

## Installing the POS App

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open Chrome → go to app.{DOMAIN}/dashboard/pos
2. Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Open from your home screen

### iOS (Safari)
1. Open Safari → go to app.{DOMAIN}/dashboard/pos
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

## First-Time Setup
When you first open the POS, it downloads your products and customers to the device (takes ~30 seconds). After that, it works offline.

## Sync Status
The sync indicator in the POS toolbar shows:
- 🟢 **Online** — connected, real-time
- 🟡 **Syncing X sales** — uploading offline sales
- 🔴 **Offline** — no connection, working locally
